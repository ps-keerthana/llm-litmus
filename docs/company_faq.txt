What is our return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy for all products. No receipt is needed for returns under 500 rupees. Items must be in original condition.

What are your working hours?
Our support team works Monday to Saturday, 9 AM to 6 PM IST. We are closed on Sundays and national holidays.

How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days. Express delivery takes 1-2 business days and costs an additional 99 rupees.

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept UPI, credit cards, debit cards, net banking, and cash on delivery. EMI is available on orders above 2000 rupees.

How do I track my order?
You can track your order by visiting our website and entering your order ID in the Track Order section. You will also receive SMS updates.

What is your cancellation policy?
Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placing them. After 2 hours, cancellation is not possible if the order has been dispatched.
