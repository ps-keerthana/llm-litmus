Architecture
4-Step RAG Evaluation Loop

From raw tax documents to graded AI answers — fully automated, zero human review required.

01
📄
Document Ingestion
8 official Indian tax guides — Sec 80C, 80D, Section 24b, HRA, TDS, NPS, Capital Gains — split into 49 semantic chunks and indexed in ChromaDB.
02
🔍
Vector Retrieval (k=5)
Each query is encoded into dense embeddings via all-MiniLM-L6-v2. Top-5 semantically relevant chunks are retrieved.
03
🤖
LLM Answer Synthesis
Llama 3.3 70B (Groq, remote) or Llama 3.2 1B (Ollama, local) synthesises a precise answer strictly from retrieved context.
04
⚖️
Automated Quality Gate
Answers are scored on semantic similarity, faithfulness, hallucination rate, and contextual recall. CI gate blocks merges below 70% pass rate.
