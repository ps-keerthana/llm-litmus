Architecture

The evaluation loop

Every query travels through a four-stage automated pipeline — from document retrieval to final verdict — with no human intervention.

Step 01
Document ingestion
8 official Indian tax guides split into 49 semantic chunks and indexed in ChromaDB with MiniLM-L6-v2 embeddings.
Step 02
Vector retrieval
Each query is embedded and matched against the vector store. Top-5 most semantically relevant chunks are retrieved.
Step 03
Answer synthesis
Llama 3.3 70B (Groq) or Llama 3.2 1B (Ollama) generates a grounded answer strictly from retrieved context.
Step 04
Quality gate
Answers are scored on semantic similarity, faithfulness, and hallucination rate. CI blocks merges below 70% pass rate.
